Ho‘ōla i nā ʻŌiwi. Ho‘oulu i nā Kaiāulu.

Strengthening Native Hawaiian well-being, growing community leaders, and creating opportunities for future generations across Hawaiʻi.

Ho‘ōla ʻŌiwi Foundation is dedicated to improving the well-being of Native Hawaiian communities through education, leadership development, maternal health, cultural stewardship, and community empowerment while welcoming all who share a commitment to Hawaiʻi's future.
