About

ʻO Wai Mākou

Ho‘ōla ʻŌiwi Foundation was created to strengthen Native Hawaiian well-being while helping communities thrive. We believe lasting change happens when culture, education, health, leadership, and community are connected.

Through collaborative partnerships and community-driven programs, we work to uplift families, support future leaders, and create opportunities that honor both our heritage and our future.
