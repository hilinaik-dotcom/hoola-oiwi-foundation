Mission

Our Mission

Hōʻola ʻŌiwi Foundation is rooted in culture, education, maternal wellness, leadership, and community care. We believe strong families and grounded Native Hawaiian, Pacific Islander, and Indigenous leaders help create a healthier future for our communities.

Our work will support learning, healing, cultural connection, and pathways that help people grow with confidence and purpose.
